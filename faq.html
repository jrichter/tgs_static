FAQ

 

How is sod sold?

Tulsa Grass & Sod Farms have individual rolls or slabs for small jobs, and pallets for larger jobs. For installation jobs, we also have big rolls which can vary in length according to the job. Spriggs are also availible in the spring, minimum quantities apply for spriggs only.

What is the size/difference between rolls and pallets?

Bermuda, Fescue, and Bluegrass are cut by the roll. Rolls are 9 sq ft, 18″ wide and 6 ft long. Zoysia is cut into slabs. Slabs are 3.75 sq ft, 18″ wide and 30″ long. Buffalograss is only cut in big rolls, dimensions vary according to need.

How many rolls/slabs are on a pallet?

There are 56 rolls on a pallet (500 Sq Ft Total), and 108 slabs on a pallet of Zoysia (400 Sq Ft Total). Do you deliver and/or install? Can I pickup my sod? Yes, we can provide both delivery and installation. Pickup is also available.

How much is delivery/installation?

Delivery varies according to ship to location. Delivery quotes are availible; call (800)658-1519 or (918)369-3662 for a quote. Installation has many factors to determine price, please call (800)658-1519 or (918)369-3662 for a free estimate.

I am laying my sod myself; how do I lay the sod?

You should lay your sod as soon as possible, especially during very warm weather. We recommend you have 3-4 inches of loose topsoil to begin. Lay the sod pieces as close as possible. It is very important to water your sod as you are laying it. Keep the ground moist for at least two weeks to establish the new lawn. Do not mow your new sod until the roots have grown into the ground completely. Please see our Lawn Care Section for more instructions.
