About

 

Ray and Patty Volentine, Sr. pioneered the sod farming industry in the state of Oklahoma when they purchased their first ten acres in 1953, creating Tulsa Grass and Sod Farms. What started as a small family farm has grown into over 1700 acres in production. Tulsa Grass and Sod Farms was the first grass/sod industry in Oklahoma to reduce the cost of grass so everyone can afford to have a nice lawn. We have consistently tested and improved sod varieties to provide the finest lawn grasses anywhere.
